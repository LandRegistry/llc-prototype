How to use the map

How to draw your search area

Click the add area button

  • choose a location on the map and click to start drawing
  • keep clicking around the edge of your search area to draw the extent
  • to complete your shape, join the ends up or double click

To draw curves

  • hold down the SHIFT key and click
  • release the SHIFT key and mouse button to stop drawing
  • or release only SHIFT to continue drawing a straight edge

Before confirming, ensure the search area captured includes all of the properties and land you want to search against, including (if necessary) driveways, alleys or gardens, etc.

How to edit your search area

Click the edit area button

  • you will see your search area turn yellow
  • click and drag the outline of your search area to edit it
  • click the edit area button again to finish editing

Transcript for: How to edit your search area

In the video, a user:

  1. clicks the edit area button
  2. sees the search area turn yellow
  3. clicks the cursor on one of the points
  4. drags the point to change the search area
  5. holds ALT whilst clicking another point to delete it
  6. clicks the edit area button again to finish editing

How to copy a building from the map

You can only copy from the map when you are zoomed in to a location (seeing a place close-up). Make sure you can see building outlines on the map.

Click the copy from map button

  • click on the building you want to copy
  • the building will be highlighted

Before confirming, ensure the search area captured includes all of the properties and land you want to search against, including (if necessary) driveways, alleys or gardens, etc. Using this feature once on a property may not always be enough to capture the whole affected area.

How to draw a shape with snap to

You can only use snap-to when you are zoomed into a location (seeing a place close-up).

Click the add area button

Click the snap to map box

  • hover your cursor near the line or point you want to snap to
  • the cursor will jump to that point automatically

This feature can also be used during other map interactions, like editing.

How to delete one search area

Click the delete area button

  • your search area will turn red
  • click the search area you want to delete

How to delete your search area

Click the clear all button

By clicking the remove button it deletes everything you have drawn on the map.

How to undo your last map interaction

Click the undo button

Your last interaction(s) with the map will be reverted.

You can click undo as many times as necessary to undo multiple actions in sequence.
