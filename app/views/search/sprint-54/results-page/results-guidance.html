Local land charge updates

London City Council

Charge Creation Dates

The creation date for all charges has been found to be inconsistent with the registration date. A policy decision has been made to align all charge creation dates to the registration date on the charge.

Before After
Creation date 01/01/2006 09/07/1994
Registration date 09/07/1994 09/07/1994

Originating Authority

Historic Originating Authorities were updated to reflect the current version (With updated address being moved to further information location, as deemed a better fit).

Originating Authority for Ancient Monument charges has been updated to “Secretary of State”.

Originating Authority for charges relating to “Highways England”, “Livv Housing Group” and “United Utilities” have been changed to the names mentioned respectively

Further Information Location

A policy decision was applied to allow customers to self-serve where possible. Based on charge specifics, customers will be directed to the appropriate website/given contact details in order to find further information.

Before After
Further Information Location This matches its respective Originating Authority. This is now Charge dependent, e.g. https://historicengland.org.uk/ for Ancient Monuments, dcsubmissions@smithfield.gov.uk for Part 3b’s etc.

Charge Types, Charge Sub-Categories and Statutory Provision

Charge Types, Charge Sub- Categories and Statutory Provisions have either been populated through an OACI list rule (where the field was blank), or re-assigned (Where the Local Authorities version and the recognised legislation differed).

The process was introduced to ensure all charge variations were aligned to a specific variable, so that the nature of the charge was easier to identify en masse.

Charge Geographic Description

Charge Geographic Descriptions have been populated for all charges (where the field was blank) or shortened (where there were multiple addresses for part 3b’s).

The process was introduced to ensure every record was assigned a single address, albeit property/land specific, master or summary.
